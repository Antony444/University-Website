about us

Director's Message

Welcome to the Arab Open University (AOU) - Egypt. Arab Open University is a non-profitable higher education institution that aims at giving equal chances to all people who seek high quality education. Its full affiliation to the Open University in UK opens for all students high-standard horizons of learning as they study the same curricula taught in the UK. The AOU, accordingly, is the only university in Egypt that grants its students an exclusive British certificate, and at highly competitive installed tuition fees. This qualifies its graduates to work worldwide. Since its inception in 2002, AOU’s mission has been to develop and disseminate knowledge, and build expertise according to international quality standards
